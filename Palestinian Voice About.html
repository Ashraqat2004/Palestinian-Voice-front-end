About Us

Welcome to Palestinian Voice

Q: Why did we create this platform?

Because Gaza deserves to be seen, heard, and understood — not just in headlines, but in truth. We built this platform to provide accurate, timely, and human-centered news from and about Gaza, for those who seek to understand beyond the noise.

Q: What do we stand for?

We stand for truth, integrity, and the voices of the people. Our mission is to document what’s happening on the ground with honesty and heart — to amplify the stories that matter and challenge misinformation with facts.

Q: Who are we?

We’re journalists, writers, and storytellers — some of us from Gaza, some of us standing in solidarity. What unites us is a deep commitment to reporting with accuracy, empathy, and context.

Q: What kind of news do we cover?

From daily developments and breaking news to deeper stories of resilience, culture, and history — we cover the full picture of life in Gaza. We aim to inform, not inflame; to educate, not sensationalize.

Sources We Rely On

We carefully select our sources to ensure credibility and transparency. Some of the main sources we rely on include:

  • Local journalists and on-the-ground correspondents in Gaza
  • Palestinian news agencies such as WAFA, Shehab News, and Quds News Network
  • Al Jazeera Network for its live coverage and in-depth analysis
  • International human rights organizations like Amnesty International and Human Rights Watch
  • Verified eyewitness accounts and open-source intelligence (OSINT)
We always strive to cross-reference and verify information before publishing.
